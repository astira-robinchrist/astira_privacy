Privacy policy

The essentials at a glance

Astira works entirely offline. The app has no internet permission and therefore cannot technically transmit any data from your device. There are no accounts, no advertising, no tracking and no analytics services. No personal data is transmitted to us or to third parties.

Location data (optional)

To display the starry sky for your location, the app can access the device location — only with your explicit consent via the Android permission prompt (legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

  • The location is used exclusively on the device to compute the sky view.
  • It is not stored permanently (only in memory during use) and not transmitted — the app has no internet access.
  • The permission is optional: you can instead enter the location manually and revoke a granted permission at any time in the Android settings.

Sensor data

In live mode the app uses motion and orientation sensors (rotation sensor, magnetometer) to determine the device's viewing direction. This data is processed exclusively in real time on the device and is neither stored nor transmitted.

Locally stored settings

The app stores your settings (e.g. night mode, brightness limit, distance unit, displayed layers, language selection, onboarding status) exclusively locally on your device. This data contains no personal information and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear the app data.

Google Play

When you obtain the app via Google Play, Google processes data as an independent controller (e.g. about installation and crashes). You can find information about this in Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy
